Who We Are?

Mototek is a UK brand, supplying quality, low cost, learner legal motorcycles and scooters from the far east, with exclusive upgrades and styling. We also supply quality spare parts for all the vehicles we supply plus many other manufacturer's models.

The stunning NEW Mototek range includes carefully selected, high quality, learner legal motorcycles and scooters, with exclusive specification upgrades and styling for the UK. The Mototek range allows customers to enjoy all the style of the leading brands at a fraction of the cost, all backed up with a range of warranty options.
