About Us / Who We Are

Welcome to Mototek,

We are an established motorcycle company who specialise in supplying quality, low cost, learner legal motorcycles and scooters from the far east, with exclusive upgrades and styling. We also supply quality spare parts for all of the vehicles that we supply, plus many other manufacturer's models.

Our aim is to offer every individual the opportunity to enjoy motorcycling on stylish, quality bikes and scooters, but at a fraction of the cost of the equivalent mainstream Japanese or European built machines

Our new range of vehicles include carefully selected, high quality, learner legel machines with exclusive specification upgrades and styling for the UK.
